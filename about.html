Our Services

  • Rapid Prototyping: From concept to functional prototype in record time. Take advantage of our expertise to test and perfect your innovations without compromising on quality. Each project is unique and deserves personalized attention to ensure its success.
  • Custom Production: Tailored solutions for all your production needs. Thanks to our ability to produce on demand, we create components and finished products with precision and consistency, adapting to your project's specifications.
  • Advanced Materials: We offer a wide range of high-quality materials, including PLA, TPU, PETG, Polycarbonate, Nylon, and more. Choose the ideal material for your project, ensuring superior performance and durability.
  • 3D Model Design: Using the most advanced parametric design software, we can render in 3D the objects in your mind. Just provide us with a drawing and measurements.
  • 3D Scanning: Using 3D scanners, we can replicate any object, whether broken or intact.
